Client side encryption

Your data are encrypted before it ever leaves the browser. We don't store your password on our server. That means that we can't decrypt your data. Ever
We use AES 256 with the Web Crypto API : The standard encryption mechanism provided by the browser.
The decryption of your data only occurs on the browser side. So the only place where your data is in the clear is on your device.
